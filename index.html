Plant Leaf Disease Classification

This project helps to reduce the human effort by classifying the plant leaf into categories like Healthy or Specific diseased categories of a leaf using Deep Learning Techniques.

CNN models used in building model are Cifar10, GoogLeNet.

This is used in proper prediction of leaf disease and can help in taking measures to save crop life and reduces the wastage of crop production and improves the quality of crops grown

It has been trained with Maize and Tomato Leaves and classifies those leaves into predefined classes it has been trained with
